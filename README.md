### **PREDICTIVE STUDIES OF BLACKFLIES IN THE IBERIAN PENINSULA**

**Description:** Creation of predictive algorithms to determine the effect of larvicidal treatments and climatic variables on the blackfly population proliferating in the Manzanares River (Madrid).

Study applied at a practical level with data provided by the Madrid City Council and personal experience in the treatment and identification of samples from a pest population in the context of performing duties as a Supervisor of Services in a pest control company.

**Key Technologies:**
* **Languages:** `R` (primarily), `HTML`
* **Methodology:** nferential statistical analysis (Tidyverse), Dimensionality Reduction through VIF analysis, Predictive Models (Random Forest, Elastic Net, Mixed Models, PERMANOVA).


## <h2 align="center">📝📈 Individual Projects 📈📝

### <h3 align="center">🐀 **Predictive biostatistical study of rat populations based on citizen reports** 🐀  [🔗 Quick access](https://github.com/samuelpintosgonzalez3a/Bioinformatica_SamuelPintos/tree/45d861b5c66c1e0bf1ddc134fb66788e408c2428/Proyectos_Individuales/Bioestad%C3%ADstica_de_Ratas)

**Description:** Data analysis from Madrid Salud technicians to record all citizen reports collected due to the presence of rats in the central area of Madrid, and its subsequent biostatistical analysis where predictive models are applied to determine the impact of environmental variables on the increase of rat reports.

**Key Technologies:**
* **Languages:** `R` (primarily), `HTML`
* **Methodology:** Inferential statistical analysis (Tidyverse, Binomial Mixed Models), Descriptive studies and classical statistics (Spearman, Kruskal-Wallis), and Machine Learning Models (Decision Trees).
