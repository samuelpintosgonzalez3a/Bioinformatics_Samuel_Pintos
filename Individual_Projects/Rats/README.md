# <h1 align="center">🐀 Predictive biostatistical study of rat populations based on citizen reports 🐀

Description: Use of data from Madrid Salud technicians to record all citizen reports collected regarding the presence of rats in central Madrid, followed by a biostatistical analysis where predictive models are applied to determine the impact of environmental variables on the increase in rat reports.

## <h2 align="center">Key Technologies:

Languages: `R` (primarily), `HTML`

Methodology: Inferential statistical analysis (`Tidyverse`, `Binomial Mixed Models`), descriptive studies and classical statistics (`Spearman`, `Kruskal-Wallis`), and Machine Learning models (`Decision Trees`).
